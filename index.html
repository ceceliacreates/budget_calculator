Definitions:
  • Essential Expenses: Expenses that are necessary and fixed (not easily changed)
  • Discretionary Expenses: Expenses that change from month to month and are extra (fun money!)
  • Utilities: Include internet and cell phone if you consider these essential expenses
  • Restaurants/Bars: Including coffee shops and fast food
  • Shopping: ALL shopping, including personal items like toiletries, gifts for others, clothes, etc.
  • Entertainment: Movies, video games, subscriptions (Spotify, Netflix), concerts, sports events, etc.
